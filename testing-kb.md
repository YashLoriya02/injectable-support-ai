## Setup & Installation

### Laptop Setup
1. Unbox your laptop and plug in the power adapter
2. Press the power button to turn it on
3. Follow the on-screen setup wizard
4. Connect to Wi-Fi and create your user account
5. Install essential software and updates
6. Transfer your files from your old device

### Smart Home Device Setup
1. Download the manufacturer's app (Alexa, Google Home, etc.)
2. Plug in your device and wait for the indicator light
3. Open the app and tap "Add Device"
4. Follow the pairing instructions
5. Connect the device to your Wi-Fi network
6. Name your device and assign it to a room

### Gaming Console Setup
1. Connect the console to your TV using HDMI
2. Plug in the power cable
3. Turn on the console and TV
4. Follow the initial setup screens
5. Connect to the internet (Wi-Fi or Ethernet)
6. Sign in to your gaming account
7. Download system updates
8. Install your first game and start playing!

## Technical Support & Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Device won't turn on**
- Check if the battery is charged
- Try a different charging cable and adapter
- Hold the power button for 10+ seconds to force restart

**Slow performance**
- Close unused apps and browser tabs
- Clear cache and temporary files
- Restart your device
- Check for software updates

**Wi-Fi connection problems**
- Restart your router and device
- Forget the network and reconnect
- Move closer to the router
- Check if other devices can connect

**Screen issues**
- Adjust brightness settings
- Disable auto-brightness temporarily
- Check for screen protector interference
- Contact support if the screen is physically damaged
